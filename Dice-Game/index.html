How to play?

  • To start the game, please, click New Game button
  • Enter the goal score for the round and choose number of dice
  • Each player rolls a dice and sum of the dice gets added to his current score
  • If the number of dice is 1, then whenever a player rolls a dice with number '1', the player losts current score and it's next player's turn
  • If the number of dice is 2, then whenever a player rolls a two dice with number '1' and '1' or '6' and '6', the players losts current score and it's next player's turn
  • A player can choose hold, which means current score will be added to the main score and it's next player's turn
  • When a player chooses to hold the current score and after summing up with main score, the main score is equal or greater than the goal score, the player wins and round ends
  • After the round ends, the global score of the winner gets incremented by 1
