Game rules


1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.


2. The GOAL score is set at the beginning by the user.


3. In each turn, a player rolls a dice and each result gets added to his ROUND score.


4. A Player loses his ROUND score when he rolls two 1s in a row and it's next Player's turn.


5. A Player loses his TOTAL score when he rolls two 6s two times in a row.


6. The Player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score and after that it's next Player's turn.


7. The first player to reach points which is set at the beginning, wins the game.
